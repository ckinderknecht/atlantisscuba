FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How much does scuba certification cost?
A: Three packages. Base is $379, Deluxe is $499, Platinum is $589. All include PADI eLearning, multiple pool sessions, and lake certification dives.

Q: How long does certification take?
A: Two weekends. The first covers three pool sessions across three days. The following weekend includes four lake certification dives, weather dependent. Deluxe/Platinum add a Thursday evening session.

Q: What gear do I need to bring vs. what's provided?
A: We provide tank, BCD, regulator, weights, and wetsuit. You supply personal fit items: mask, snorkel, fins, and boots. Students get 10% off these items plus $50 off tuition if purchased from us.

Q: What pool do you use in DFW?
A: Indoor, heated Olympic-sized pool in Southlake with 3-foot shallow end, 5-8-foot mid-section, and 13-foot diving well.

Q: What's the student-to-instructor ratio?
A: We target 4:1 in the pool and lake. With younger students or challenging conditions, it's 2:1 or 1:1.

Q: What certification agency do you use?
A: PADI, the world's most widely recognized scuba certification with nearly 60 years of history. Cards recognized by operators worldwide.

Q: What is PADI eLearning?
A: Digital manual with embedded videos and self-paced exams ($250 value, included). Takes about 9 hours, completed on your schedule before pool sessions.

Q: What happens at the lake?
A: Four certification dives over two days, typically Saturday morning and Sunday afternoon. Your Open Water certification is completed here.

Q: Do you offer private lessons?
A: Yes. Private instruction and additional practice sessions available on request. Perfect for skill refinement or advanced development.

Q: Do you service equipment and provide air fills?
A: Yes. We service major regulator and BCD brands, provide air fills, and perform hydrostatic and visual tank testing.

Q: Do you offer dive travel?
A: Yes. We offer robust dive travel from DFW. We have guides who know destinations both above and below water.
